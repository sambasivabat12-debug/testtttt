# 🦷 Sri Sai Speciality Dental Care App

> *"Beauty is power and smile its sword"*

A full-featured dental clinic management app for **Sri Sai Speciality Dental Care (T V Rao)**, Hyderabad.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🏠 **Dashboard** — Today's appointments, stats, schedule management
- 📅 **Book Appointment** — 4-step booking with WhatsApp confirmation
- 🦷 **Tooth Chart** — Interactive 32-tooth dental chart with conditions & notes
- 💳 **Payments** — Invoice management with UPI, PhonePe, GPay, Razorpay
- 🤖 **AI Assistant** — Claude-powered dental chatbot
- 💊 **Prescriptions** — Digital Rx creation, printing & WhatsApp sharing
- 🖼️ **Gallery** — Before & after treatment cases
- 👤 **Patient Portal** — Login, history, appointments, prescriptions
- 📷 **Reports** — Upload & manage X-rays and dental reports
- 🏥 **About / Doctors** — Clinic info and doctor profiles

## 🚀 Deploy on Vercel (Step by Step)

### Step 1 — Upload to GitHub
1. Go to [github.com](https://github.com) → Sign up / Login
2. Click **"+"** → **"New repository"**
3. Name it: `sri-sai-dental-app` → Click **"Create repository"**
4. Upload ALL these files to the repository

### Step 2 — Deploy on Vercel
1. Go to [vercel.com](https://vercel.com)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** → **"Continue with GitHub"**
3. Click **"Add New Project"**
4. Select `sri-sai-dental-app` → Click **"Import"**
5. Click **"Deploy"** 🚀
6. Wait 1–2 minutes → Your app is LIVE! 🎉

### Your Live URL will be:
```
https://sri-sai-dental-app.vercel.app
```

## 📱 Install on Phone (PWA)

### Android
1. Open the URL in Chrome
2. Tap the **3-dot menu** → **"Add to Home Screen"**
3. Tap **"Add"** → App icon appears on your home screen!

### iPhone
1. Open the URL in Safari
2. Tap the **Share button** (box with arrow)
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"** → App icon appears!
